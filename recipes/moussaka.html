The tastiest moussaka recipe

Moussaka is to the Greek what Lasagna is to Italians. A rich tomato meat sauce layered with eggplant instead of pasta sheets, and topped with a thick layer of bechamel sauce, this traditional Greek recipe takes time to assemble but it is well worth the effort!

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Potato

Steps

  1. Cooking the eggplant
  2. The meat sauce, a rich Bolognese type sauce made with lamb or beef but with traditional Greek flavours of oregano and cinnamon
  3. thick bechamel sauce thicker than used in Lasagna and things like Broccoli Gratin, it is semi-set using eggs
  4. Layering it all up, lasagna style
